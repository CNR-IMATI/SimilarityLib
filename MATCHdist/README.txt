This executable computes the matching distance between two persistence diagrams

Usage:  matchCrtPts.exe file1 file2 output par1 par2 where:
file1 and file2: are two persistence diagrams; 
output: output file name;
par1 and par2: are two parameters on the weights of the two diagrams, as defaults they are set at 1 0.
